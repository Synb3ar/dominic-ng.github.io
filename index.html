Professional Bio

Dominic Ng is a second-year BSc Management and Information Technology student at Lancaster University, predicted a 2:1, with a strong interest in financial analysis, data analytics, and business reporting. Through internships and coursework, he has developed hands-on experience working with Excel-based reporting, dashboard creation, and structured data analysis to support business decision-making.

Dominic has completed a Finance Internship at Qabas Consulting & Training, where he worked with data and reports using Microsoft Excel, coordinated activities remotely, and managed multiple tasks while maintaining accuracy and attention to detail. He also participated in insight programmes at Kainos and IBM, where he led a team to design a functional website and collaborated on a sustainability-focused product pitch.

His recent academic projects include credit risk analysis dashboards, workforce retention and HR analytics dashboards, and bank reconciliation with financial reporting models in Excel. These projects reflect his ability to turn complex data into clear, actionable insights.

Beyond finance, Dominic has over four years of customer-facing experience in hospitality environments at Lancaster University and The Joyinn, Belfast, where he developed strong communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills under pressure.
